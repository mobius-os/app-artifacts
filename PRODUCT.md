# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Möbius owners browsing interactive HTML artifacts created by their in-product agent. They move between chats and the catalog to inspect, revisit, version, and share finished work on phones and larger screens.

## Product Purpose

Artifacts makes agent-created interactive pages durable and easy to navigate. Success means an owner can immediately find the right artifact, understand its provenance and version history, safely run it, return to its chat, and manage a stable public snapshot without exposing app credentials.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, capable, and trustworthy. The interface should feel native to Möbius: simple enough to disappear behind the artifact while remaining precise around provenance, versioning, sharing, and destructive actions.

## Anti-references

Avoid dashboard ornament, decorative gradients, glass effects, novelty controls, tiny targets, and custom colors that compete with owner-selected Möbius themes. Never make artifact content look like trusted shell UI.

## Design Principles

- Put the artifact first and keep catalog chrome restrained.
- Make provenance, version, and share state legible at a glance.
- Use familiar, reversible interaction patterns with explicit failure feedback.
- Preserve a safe boundary between agent-authored HTML and owner credentials.
- Adapt structurally from a focused mobile flow to efficient wider layouts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA expectations: semantic controls, visible keyboard focus, 44px minimum targets, readable type and contrast through platform tokens, reduced-motion support, screen-reader labels, and layouts that remain usable at narrow widths and browser zoom.
